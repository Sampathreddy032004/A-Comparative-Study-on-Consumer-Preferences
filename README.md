
**Business Analytics Project: A Comparative Study on Consumer Preferences**

---

### Project Description

* Conducted a comparative analysis of two leading Indian pharmacies, MedPlus and Apollo, focusing on business operations, service delivery, and overall customer satisfaction.


* Utilized a descriptive research design and a simple random sampling method to collect and evaluate primary data from 149 respondents.


* Designed and deployed a structured questionnaire featuring multiple-choice questions to measure key metrics, including consumer satisfaction, product preferences, and service quality.


* Executed statistical data analysis, specifically utilizing two-sample t-Tests assuming unequal variances in Microsoft Excel, to test hypotheses regarding consumer satisfaction levels.


* Identified key drivers of consumer behavior, concluding that MedPlus attracts customers through competitive pricing and discounts, while Apollo is preferred for its trustworthiness and high-quality customer service.
